javaBin is open to anyone who wants to become a member. Every membership supports the Java community in Norway and helps us to organize various events throughout the year, including JavaZone.

How much does a membership cost?

Membership in javaBin is included in the JavaZone ticket, so if you have bought a ticket to JavaZone or have received this from your employer, then you are already a member of javaBin for that year. If you are not going to JavaZone, you can buy a separate 12-month membership for 600 NOK.

Students with a valid student ID can apply for free membership. If you are interested in a student membership, please send an email to medlemskap@java.no.

Validity

Membership is valid from the day it was purchased to the same date next year. This applies for both memberships accompanying JavaZone tickets, and for regular memberships.
